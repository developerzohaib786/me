How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline for developing a website varies depending on its complexity and features. A simple website may take 1-3 weeks, while more complex projects like e-commerce platforms, custom applications, or real estate websites can take 6-12 weeks or longer. The process typically includes planning, design, development, testing, and launch. We will provide a detailed timeline after understanding your project requirements.

How much does it cost to build a website?

The cost of building a website depends on several factors, including the number of pages, design complexity, functionality (e.g., blogs, e-commerce, user accounts), and any custom features you need. After discussing your goals and project details, we can provide an accurate quote tailored to your specific requirements.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and SEO-optimized?

Yes, absolutely! All websites we develop are fully responsive, meaning they work smoothly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Additionally, we implement best practices for search engine optimization (SEO) to help your site rank higher in search results, ensuring better visibility and user experience.
